About Gordo

Gordo, also known as Old Reliable, was one of the first monkeys that had been launched into space. He was part of NASA's space team. However, 8 other monkeys went to space before him. Gordo was launched from Cape Canaveral on December 13th, 1958.1

Why was Gordo sent to space?

He was sent to space because of his species' similar anatomical makeup to man and sensitivity to changes in temperature. - quote by Wikipedia

Image of Gordo the Monkey

Other facts about Gordo's Mission

  1. Gordo was launched from the Jupiter AM-13 Rocket
  2. The flight lasted about 15 minutes
  3. When the flight took off the rocket reached to a height of 290 miles
