About Gordo

Gordo, also known as Old Reliable, was one of the first monkeys that had been launched into space. He was part of NASA's space team. Gordo was launched from Cape Canaveral on December 13th, 1958.

Why was Gordo sent to space?

He was sent to space because of his species' similar anatomical makeup to man and sensitivity to changes in temperature. - quote by Wikipedia
